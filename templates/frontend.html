Depression is a very real illness

Globally, it is estimated that around 5.0% of the population suffer from depression. That equates to approximately 280 million people. In many cases, depression if left undetected can lead to suicide.

There are many factors which cause depression such as..

  • Too much pressure at work

  • Losing a loved one

  • Disturbed sleep cycle

  • Financial problems

  • Hormones and the list goes on..

But the good news is that, depression is a very treatable illness if it is identified in time and the patient seeks help from external sources such as


  • Therapy

  • Antidepressant medications such as Esketamine, Norepinephrine, etc

  • Fixing sleep cycle, diet (eating more healthy vitamin rich foods)

  • Adding exercise to lifestyle which boosts self-confidence
